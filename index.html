Interesting Facts about the Camel

  • To keep sand out of their eyes, camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes.
  • Camels can reach seven feet in height and weight up to fifteen thousand pounds.
  • Camels are espicially adapted to life in the desert.

Interesting Facts about the Panda

  • Pandas have great camoflauge for their environment.
  • Bamboo is crtical to pandas diet, but they occasionally eat something other than bamboo.
  • Pandas thick black and white fur helps them with life in cool forests.

Interesting Facts about the Bear

  • Bears are very intelligent animals. They have better navigation skills than humans.
  • Bears care deeply for other animals, espicially their family.
  • Bears have excellent hearing, seeing and smelling senses.

Interesting Facts about the Pigeon

  • Pigeons can find their way back to the nest from 1300 miles away.
  • Pigeons are very sociable.
  • Pigeons have outstanding navigation abilities.
